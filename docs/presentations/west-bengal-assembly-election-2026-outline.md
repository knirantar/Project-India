# Presentation Outline: West Bengal Assembly Election 2026

## Audience

Project India readers who want to understand why the Bengal verdict matters for state politics, national politics, governance, and India's federal future.

## Core Message

The 2026 Bengal election is not just a state result. It is a major political realignment that could reshape eastern India, BJP's national map, TMC's future, and the governance model of one of India's most important states.

## Slide Outline

1. Title: West Bengal Assembly Election 2026
2. The verdict in one chart: BJP 207, TMC 80, others to verify
3. Why Bengal matters: population, border, culture, economy, federal politics
4. Timeline: polling, counting, Falta repoll issue, oath ceremony
5. From 2021 to 2026: BJP's seat jump and TMC's collapse
6. Key personalities: Suvendu Adhikari, Mamata Banerjee, BJP central leadership
7. Geography of the result: north Bengal, south Bengal, Kolkata, border districts, industrial belts
8. Possible drivers: anti-incumbency, welfare politics, identity, organization, candidate selection
9. Risks: violence, polarization, over-centralization, administrative transition
10. Opportunities: governance reset, infrastructure, industry, center-state alignment
11. What to watch in the first 100 days
12. Open questions and data gaps

## Speaker Notes To Build

- Keep official ECI numbers separate from early media reports.
- Avoid reducing the result to one cause.
- Use constituency data before making claims about social blocs.
- Track what BJP changes, continues, or renames from TMC governance.
