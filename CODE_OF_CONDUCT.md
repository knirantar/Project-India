# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Project India is committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all contributors and participants to uphold the following principles:

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Be Respectful**: Treat others with respect, regardless of their background, expertise, or viewpoint
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome diverse perspectives, experiences, and ideas
- **Be Constructive**: Provide feedback that is helpful, specific, and kind
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together toward shared goals of understanding and research
- **Be Honest**: Acknowledge uncertainty, cite sources, and correct mistakes gracefully

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile communication
- Disrespect toward contributors or their work
- Spreading misinformation or unsourced claims
- Personal attacks or inflammatory language
- Excluding individuals based on background or identity

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Project India spaces: repositories, discussions, issues, and external events organized by the project.
